Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At LearnFlow, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Key Point: Most of your data is stored locally in your browser. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

LearnFlow collects minimal information to provide our services:

  • Account Information - Name and email when you sign up
  • Learning Progress - Quiz results, flashcard decks, and study history
  • Chat History - Conversations with the AI tutor (stored locally on your device)
  • Usage Data - How you interact with the platform to improve our services

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized learning experiences
  • To track your progress and achievements
  • To improve our AI and services
  • To send important updates about your account (if you opt in)

Data Storage

Most of your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • Clearing browser data will remove your local progress
  • We cannot access your locally stored data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • No storage of sensitive payment information
  • Regular security reviews

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View your personal data at any time
  • Delete - Remove your account and all associated data
  • Export - Download your learning data
  • Opt-out - Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Cookies

LearnFlow uses essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide core functionality

Children's Privacy

LearnFlow is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
